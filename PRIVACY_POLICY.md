# Privacy Policy

This extension does not collect, store, sell, or transmit any personal user data.

The extension operates entirely locally in the user's browser and processes only publicly available content from the Shikimori website in order to provide its functionality, such as displaying an embedded player and generating related external links.

All data processing performed by the extension is done locally on the user's device. The extension does not collect or transmit personal data to the developer.

The extension may load content from third-party services (such as AniLibria or Kodik) directly in the user's browser. Any data processing or analytics performed by these third-party services is subject to their respective privacy policies and is not controlled by the extension.

The use of information by this extension complies with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact [the developer](mailto:ErickaJanie198515817@outlook.com).
